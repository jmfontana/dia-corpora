List of available corpora
American English:

Corpus 1 (COCA)

http://corptedig-glif.upf.edu/cqpweb/coca/ (user: guest, pwd: guest)
Complete tagset: http://www.natcorp.ox.ac.uk/docs/c7spec.html

Important note: The tagset is almost identical to the one used by Sketchengine but in this corpus you need to use [pos=".*"] instead of [tag=".*"] in the CQL query and use lower case for the POS tags.
Select the option 'CQP Syntax' in the 'Query mode' menu

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [pos="n.*"]
Verbs ➝ [pos="vv.*"]
Determiners ➝ [pos="dd.*"]
Articles ➝ [pos="at.*"]
Adjectives ➝ [pos="j.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [pos="i.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [pos="pp.*"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [pos="rp"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

British English:

Corpus 2 (BNC in Sketchengine)

https://app.sketchengine.eu/ (choose British National Corpus - BNC)
Complete tagset: https://www.sketchengine.eu/english-treetagger-pipeline-2/

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [tag="N.*"]
Verbs ➝ [tag="VV.*"]
Determiners (includes articles) ➝ [tag="DT"]
Adjectives ➝ [tag="J.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [tag="IN.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [tag="PP"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [tag="RP"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

http://corptedig-glif.upf.edu/cqpweb/bnc/ (user: guest, pwd: guest)
Complete tagset: https://www.sketchengine.eu/english-claws5-part-of-speech-tagset/

Important note: the tagset used in this corpus is very similar (not completely identical, though) to the one used in Sketchengine. The most important difference is that to do searches you need to use the label 'pos' instead of 'tag'. Select the option 'CQP Syntax' in the 'Query mode' menu

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [pos="N.*"]
Verbs ➝ [pos="VV.*"]
Determiners ➝ [pos="DT0"]
Articles ➝ [pos="AT0"]
Adjectives ➝ [pos="J.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [pos="PR.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [pos="PN.*"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [pos="RP"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

Some shortcuts to make your life easier:

A Noun Phrase:

Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

[(pos="dd.*")|(pos="at.*")]{0,1}[pos="j.*"]{0,1}[pos="n.*"]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

[tag="DT"]{0,1}[tag="J.*"]{0,1}[tag="N.*"]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

[(pos="DT0")|(pos="AT0")]{0,1}[pos="J.*"]{0,2}[pos="N.*"]

A choice between two lexical categories (either one or the other):

Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

[(pos="x.*")|(pos="x.*")]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

[tag="DT"]{0,1}[tag="J.*"]{0,1}[tag="N.*"]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

[(pos="DT0")|(pos="AT0")]{0,1}[pos="J.*"]{0,2}[pos="N.*"]
