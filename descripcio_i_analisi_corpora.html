List of available corpora

### Review tags, some of the tags used in the tagsets for the UPF corpora might be incorrect
American English:

Corpus 1 (COCA)

http://corptedig-glif.upf.edu/cqpweb/coca/ (user: guest, pwd: guest)
Make sure you select the option 'CQP Syntax' in the 'Query mode' menu

Important note: The tagset is almost identical to the one used by Sketchengine but in this corpus you need to use [pos=".*"] instead of [tag=".*"] in the CQL query and use lower case for the POS tags.

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [pos="n.*"]
Verbs ➝ [pos="vv.*"]
Determiners ➝ [pos="dd.*"]
Articles ➝ [pos="at.*"]
Adjectives ➝ [pos="j.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [pos="i.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [pos="pp.*"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [pos="rp"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

Complete tagset: http://www.natcorp.ox.ac.uk/docs/c7spec.html

British English:

Corpus 2 (BNC in Sketchengine)

https://app.sketchengine.eu/ (choose British National Corpus - BNC)

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [tag="N.*"]
Verbs ➝ [tag="VV.*"]
Determiners (includes articles) ➝ [tag="DT"]
Adjectives ➝ [tag="J.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [tag="IN.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [tag="PP"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [tag="RP"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

Complete tagset: https://www.sketchengine.eu/english-treetagger-pipeline-2/

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

Make sure you select the option 'CQP Syntax' in the 'Query mode' menu
http://corptedig-glif.upf.edu/cqpweb/bnc/ (user: guest, pwd: guest)

Important note: the tagset used in this corpus is very similar (not completely identical, though) to the one used in Sketchengine. The most important difference is that to do searches you need to use the label 'pos' instead of 'tag'.

List of relevant tags:

Nouns ➝ [pos="N.*"]
Verbs ➝ [pos="VV.*"]
Determiners ➝ [pos="DT0"]
Articles ➝ [pos="AT0"]
Adjectives ➝ [pos="AJ.*"]
Prepositions ➝ [pos="PR.*"]
Pronouns ➝ [pos="PN.*"]
Particles (e.g. about, in, out, up) ➝ [pos="RP"] (relevant for phrasal verbs)

Complete tagset: https://www.sketchengine.eu/english-claws5-part-of-speech-tagset/

Some shortcuts to make your life easier:

Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

A Noun Phrase ➝ [(pos="dd.*")|(pos="at.*")]{0,1}[pos="j.*"]{0,1}[pos="n.*"]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

A Noun Phrase ➝ [tag="DT"]{0,1}[tag="J.*"]{0,1}[tag="N.*"]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

A Noun Phrase ➝ [(pos="DT0")|(pos="AT0")]{0,1}[pos="AJ.*"]{0,2}[pos="N.*"]


Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

Anything that's not a Det + Adj + N ➝ [pos!="dd.*"][pos="j.*"]{0,1}[pos="n.*"]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

Anything that's not a Det + Adj + N ➝ [tag!="DT"][tag="J.*"]{0,2}[tag="N.*"]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

Anything that's not a Det + Adj + N ➝ [pos!="DT0"][pos="J.*"]{0,2}[pos="N.*"]


Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

Either x or y ➝ [(pos="x.*")|(pos="y.*")]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

Either x or y ➝ [(tag="X.*")|(tag="X.*")]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

Either x or y ➝ [(pos="X.*")|(pos="X.*")]


Corpus 1 (COCA UPF)

Lexical category x and Lemma y ➝ [(pos="x.*")&(lemma="y")]

Corpus 2 (BNC sketchengine)

Lexical category x and Lemma y ➝ [(tag="x.*")&(tag="x.*")]

Corpus 3 (BNC UPF)

Lexical category x and Lemma y ➝ [(pos="X.*")&(lemma="y.*")]
